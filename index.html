About

I am a PhD Research Scholar (Supervisor: Dr. Sathishkumar Murugesan, Research Assistant Professor) at the Department of Mathematics, SRM Institute of Science and Technology, Kattankulathur Campus, India. My research focuses on Control Theory, specifically exploring Optimal Control and Dynamical Systems through the lens of Clifford (Geometric) Algebra and using Learning-based algorithms.

Experience
