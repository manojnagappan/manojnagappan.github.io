Q-LEARNING VEHICLE BATTLE

This simulation demonstrates a simple reinforcement learning system where an AI-controlled red vehicle learns to hunt the player using Q-learning.

OBJECTIVE

  • The RED vehicle is the AI agent.
  • The GREEN vehicle is the player.
  • If the AI shoots the player before the timer ends → AI wins.
  • If the player survives until time expires → Player wins.

PLAYER CONTROLS

  • ↑ : Accelerate
  • ↓ : Reverse
  • ← : Rotate Left
  • → : Rotate Right

HOW THE AI LEARNS

The AI uses Q-learning. It stores values for actions in different states and updates them after every interaction.

Positive rewards increase behaviors that help the AI win. Negative rewards discourage poor actions.

Q-TABLE MEMORY

The AI memory is stored locally inside your browser using localStorage. Refreshing the page will keep the learned data unless you clear the browser storage or press RESET AI.

NOTES

  • The AI starts dumb and improves over time.
  • Learning persists only on your browser/device.
  • No server or backend is used.
  • The AI state-space is intentionally small for fast learning.
